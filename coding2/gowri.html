About me

As a creative kid, I was always drawn to the process of making things better and making better things. During my studies in architecture I started to truly grasp the impact of design on both individuals and communities. While my studies in architecture provided me with a profound understanding of design principles, I discovered a growing fascination with product design. I was attracted by the unique challenges and opportunities it presented.

I actively engage in diverse creative outlets such as digital art, reading, watching thought-provoking movies and TV shows, and traveling. I also find joy in learning about wildlife and observing nature. These hobbies inspire me and provide a grounding force in both my professional and personal life, fueling my creativity and offering fresh perspectives.

So after my final year, I started working on developing my skills. As part of this journey, I learned the fundamentals of HTML and CSS, which allowed me to create the website you are currently viewing.

As I continue to grow and refine my craft, I look forward to the ongoing exploration and discovery that design offers, and the opportunity to bring my creative vision to life.
